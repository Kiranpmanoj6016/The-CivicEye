CIVIC EYE

Empowering Citizens for a Better Nation

Our Purpose

CIVIC EYE is a crowdsourced civic issue reporting and resolution system designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local government. We provide a streamlined, mobile-first platform that empowers community members to report everyday civic issues in real-time.

Motivation

Local governments often struggle with inefficient and slow reporting mechanisms for issues. Our motivation is to provide a modern, easy-to-use solution that makes reporting and resolving these issues simple, transparent, and effective.

Expected Impact

By providing an effortless way for citizens to submit issues, we expect to see a significant increase in civic engagement, leading to faster response times, more efficient resource allocation, and a higher level of government accountability.

Key Features

  • Live Location Tagging: Effortlessly report issues with automatic GPS location and a map-based interface.
  • Dynamic Dashboards: Dedicated portals for citizens, administrators, and department staff with real-time data.
  • Issue Hotspots: The admin map highlights priority areas where multiple issues are clustered.
  • Staff Management: Administrators can assign issues to staff and track their performance.
  • Real-Time Updates: Citizens receive live notifications when the status of their reports change.
